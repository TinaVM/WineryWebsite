Organization

The Red & White Winery is a South African company that was founded by 3 friends in Stellenbosch Western Cape back in 2013. The organisation is still fairly new but has grown exponentially in just a span of 8 years and is one of the biggest producers and distributors of red and white wine in Stellenbosch today. We take pride in the way that we care for our vineyards and hold such a diligent team of workers that are ready to serve.

Our history

1993 - In July 23rd, 3 friends Brenda Millian, Julian Gomes and Samantha Fourie founded a winery in Stellenbosch, Western Cape which produced 158,653 gallons of wine in its first year.

1994 - The Red & White Winery sold just 345,200 gallons of wine in its second year of operation.

1997 - The Winery was able to attract local agricultural investing company "SureAgri" and managed to secure the GoldBerg Ranch.

1998 - Bottling operations started within GoldBerg Ranch and The Red & White Winery trademark was registered in all nine provinces.

1999 - Partnered with Cliffendale Distributors and distributed 10,000 batches of red and white wine across local stores in the Western Cape.

2002 - Sales volumes have grown exponentially and competes with local major wineries in Stellenbosch and surrounding areas.

2006 - The Red & White Winery is recognized as the largest winery in Stellenbosch based on sales volume in the last 4 years.

2008 - Winery signs contract with GrowNow company for the planting of specific grapes.

2009 - Sparkling wine range is introduced and first 100 batch is sold in stores across Western Cape province.

2011 - The Red & White Winery's first public wine cellar is opened in Cape Town.

2012 - Winery appears on the front page of "Business10" magazine with an article titled "An Idea Turning Water Into Wine."

2014 - First official office is opened in Johannesburg and purchased 2 enoteca's.

2017 - Recognized as "Best Local Winery" of the year by The Citizen.

2018 - Bullock Vineyard is purchased in Cape Point.

2020 - The Red & White Winery celebrates 27 years of successful business.

Mission

We believe that with our diligent team of workers, we are able to uphold quality and professionalism with the highest level of expertise in order to satisfy our clients.

At The Red & White Winery, we want to make one of the best wines while strengthening our community and to intensify the satisfaction of our clients.
