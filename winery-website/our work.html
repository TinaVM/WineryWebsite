The following are the highlights of our main projects:

The GoldBerg Acquisition

The acquisition of the GoldBerg Ranch with 3000 tons of grapes in which were successfully picked by the mechanical grape harvester and tested for quality.

Cliffendale Distributors Partnership

Agreement to distribute wines on behalf of The Red & White Winery with the goal of 5000 bottles of red and white wine distributions. The winery distributed 10000 bottles in its first year of partnership.

GrowNow Long-Term Grape Contract

The project being undertaken is a long term agreement between The Red & White Winery and GrowNow company to grow specific types of grapes with hopes to introduce new ranges to our wine collection. These grapes should include a volume of 2500 tons to be ready for production processes.

Livington Farm Lease

This project involves the long term rental of 50% of the land of WCW Farming Trust in Cape Point. The lease is a period of 30 years which was agreed upon with the owner of the land and in which its use will be to develop a vineyard.

The Red & White Winery has put in place an operational farming agreement to start the growing of the vineyard and of which to produce 2000 tons of grapes once the development is complete and in full production. This will provide job opportunities in the community of Cape Point whilst adding to our workforce.
